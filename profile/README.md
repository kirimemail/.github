# KirimEmail Organization

Welcome to the KirimEmail GitHub organization. We provide tools and SDKs to help you integrate email services into your applications.

## SMTP SDKs

Our official SMTP SDKs make it easy to send emails through KirimEmail's reliable SMTP service:

- **Python SDK**: https://github.com/kirimemail/kirimemail-smtp-python-sdk
- **Node.js SDK**: https://github.com/kirimemail/kirimemail-smtp-node-sdk
- **PHP SDK**: https://github.com/kirimemail/kirimemail-smtp-php-sdk

## Other Repositories

This organization may contain additional repositories for tools, utilities, and examples related to email services and KirimEmail products.

## About KirimEmail

Kirim.Email provides reliable email delivery services, including SMTP functionality for transactional emails. Our SDKs and tools help developers easily integrate email capabilities into their applications.
